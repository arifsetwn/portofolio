I am a lecturer in Informatics Education at Universitas Muhammadiyah Surakarta, focusing on learning development, research, and innovation in educational technology. In my research, I explore the use of technology in education, including Chatbots, Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality, E-Learning, and the implementation of IoT systems for Smart Classrooms. I am passionate about integrating technical and pedagogical aspects to create practical solutions that bring real benefits to universities and partner schools.
